📚 YouTube Safe Content Guidelines

✓ Always Safe

  • Educational content with factual accuracy
  • Entertainment without harmful stereotypes
  • Music from royalty-free sources (YouTube Audio Library, Epidemic Sound)
  • Stock footage from Pexels, Pixabay, Storyblocks
  • AI-generated content with proper disclosure

⚠ Review Carefully

  • True crime — focus on facts, avoid glorifying violence
  • News — maintain accuracy, avoid misinformation
  • Finance — don't give specific investment advice
  • Health — include disclaimer, not medical advice
  • Controversial topics — balanced perspective required

✗ Avoid Completely

  • Copyrighted music without license
  • Misleading thumbnails/titles (clickbait violations)
  • Hateful or discriminatory content
  • Graphic violence or disturbing imagery
  • Bulk AI content with no original value
